Portfolio

I am an artist who works with both 2D and 3D art. I love making sci-fi artwork and 3D models using Blender and Photoshop. Scroll to see what I think is out there!

Commissions are open. You can contact me on Twitter, Instagram and through this site by scrolling to the bottom.

For portraits, I use photoshop and references images to collect the color pallete and structure. For my 3D models, I use sketches from various angles (front and side) for modelling to keep the proportions intact. For sci-fi environments, I create backdrops with Blender, after which I add details and the foreground with Photoshop.
